---
name: Brand Voice and UI Tone Rule
description: Premium UI/UX must stay separate from boutique studio copy tone
type: feedback
---

Upgrade the interface to large-tech-agency quality, but keep the writing like a sharp, focused, small studio speaking directly and honestly.

**Why:** The site should feel like a top product studio visually, but sound like a trusted human expert — not a corporate agency or inflated SaaS company. These are separate axes.

**How to apply:** When writing or editing copy, always prefer calm, direct, intelligent language. No buzzwords, no inflated claims, no "we transform brands through innovation." When designing UI, aim for Apple × Vercel × premium B2B quality — intentional spacing, strong hierarchy, subtle depth, restrained motion.

If forced to choose: preserve the small-agency voice over any enterprise-sounding language.
